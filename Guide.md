# WhatsApp Ban Tools Guide

## How to Pair and Link Your WhatsApp Device

### Step 1: Open WhatsApp
- Launch the WhatsApp application on your mobile device
- Ensure you have a stable internet connection

### Step 2: Access Settings
- Tap the **Menu** icon (three dots) in the top right corner
- Select **Linked Devices** or **Connected Devices**

### Step 3: Add a New Device
- Tap the **"+ button"** or **"Link a Device"** option
- A QR code scanner will appear on your screen

### Step 4: Scan the QR Code
- On your computer or web browser, open WhatsApp Web (web.whatsapp.com)
- Point your phone camera at the QR code displayed on the screen
- Keep the QR code in frame until it scans successfully

### Step 5: Confirm Linking
- Once scanned, your device will be linked automatically
- You should see a confirmation message on both devices
- Your WhatsApp will now be accessible from the linked device

### Important Notes
- Keep your phone connected to the internet for the linked device to work
- You can link up to 4 devices simultaneously
- Only use official WhatsApp applications
- For security, always unlink devices when not in use

### Troubleshooting
- **QR Code won't scan:** Try reloading the page or restarting the app
- **Connection lost:** Ensure your phone has an active internet connection
- **Can't find Linked Devices:** Update WhatsApp to the latest version
